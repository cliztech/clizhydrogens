# Definition of Done: Content

All content (copy, images, metadata) must meet these standards before publishing.

## 1. Voice & Tone
- [ ] **Cheeky**: Is it fun/irreverent without being offensive?
- [ ] **Direct**: Avoid fluff. Get to the point.
- [ ] **On-Brand**: Aligns with the "Cheeky Prints" persona.

## 2. SEO & Metadata
- [ ] **Title Tags**: Unique and under 60 characters.
- [ ] **Meta Descriptions**: Compelling summary under 160 characters.
- [ ] **Keywords**: Primary keyword included naturally.
- [ ] **Alt Text**: All images have descriptive alt text.

## 3. Readability
- [ ] **Grade Level**: Targeted at Grade 6-8 reading level.
- [ ] **Formatting**: Short paragraphs, bullet points (using monospace font).
- [ ] **Grammar**: No spelling or grammatical errors (unless intentional styling).
